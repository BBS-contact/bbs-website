1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Big Begins Small (BBS) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

We process your data only for the following legitimate purposes:

4. Legal Basis

Our data processing is based on user consent, legitimate interest, or contractual necessity. You may withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Data Storage and Protection

We store data securely using encryption, limited access, and regular audits. Data is not stored longer than necessary for its intended purpose.

6. Sharing Data

We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law or you have explicitly consented.

7. User Rights

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me

Our system may use essential cookies for basic functionality. We do not track users for advertising or profiling.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: bbs.contactproton.me@proton.me
