Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the BBS initiative

What is BBS?

BBS (Big Begins Small) is a global movement and platform that puts the human being at the center. We believe that change starts small: one word, one action, one choice. Through these simple but profound steps, we can transform the world.

Is BBS a political or religious organization?

No. We are not affiliated with any political party, religion, or ideology. We unite people based on ethics, respect, collaboration, and humanity — regardless of beliefs.

Can BBS be trusted?

We strive for full transparency. All donations, actions, initiatives, and partners are published in an open format. Administrative tools (Dashboard) allow tracking changes, managing security, and controlling role-based access.

Is this really achievable?

We work step-by-step in collaboration with experts, artists, lawyers, teachers, doctors, and activists. This is not utopia — it’s the practice of a new approach. We start small, because that’s how real change happens.

How can I join?

Visit the Join page, fill out a simple form, and choose the direction you want to contribute to. We welcome everyone — regardless of profession, age, or experience.

How can I help if I have no money?

The greatest value is people. Your ideas, participation, inspiration, even sharing our message on social media — that is already great support.

How are donations used?

Each donation is directed to:

  • development of tools (the platform);
  • launching projects in the fields of education, medicine, human rights, ecology, and more.

Can I start a local BBS initiative in my city?

Yes! We encourage it. You can start a local group or initiative based on our principles. Just write to us and we’ll help with the first steps.

Are personal data stored?

We care about privacy. All data is currently stored only locally (on your device) during testing. After launch, secure storage will be implemented.

Didn't find your question?

Write to us, and we will respond personally or add it here!
