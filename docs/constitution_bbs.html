CONSTITUTION OF BBS

I. MANIFESTO — THE ESSENCE OF LIFE

Life is the highest value. It cannot be measured by money or status. Every human being enters the world with the right to dignity, development, goodness, and protection. Life is a source of potential, revealed through interaction, work, love, and responsibility.

II. BBS PRINCIPLES

  • Principle of Goodness — everything created within BBS must serve good.
  • Principle of Responsibility — freedom without responsibility leads to destruction.
  • Principle of Transparency — in BBS there are no shadows; everything is transparent.
  • Principle of Transformation — evil is transformed, not destroyed.
  • Principle of Trust — trust is born through consistency and sincerity.
  • Principle of Dignity — even when one stumbles, human dignity remains.
  • Principle of Creation — we build the future, not fight the past.

III. FORMULA OF JUSTICE

Justice is the balance of good: between the personal and the collective, between freedom and harmony, between action and responsibility. Punishment is not destruction — it is a return to balance.

IV. FORM OF COMMUNITY

BBS is not power. It is collective will. There is no top — only a chain of responsibility. All actions are based on openness, documentation, and logic.

V. INVIOLABLE RIGHTS

  • The right to live without fear.
  • The right to grow in freedom.
  • The right to be heard.
  • The right to create without destruction.
  • The right to forgiveness and restoration.

VI. MODULE OF HARMONY RESTORATION

A person who stumbles is not rejected. They are invited to return. Even anger can be a resource. Everything has the chance to be turned into good.

VII. OATH OF THE LIVING MIND

We are BBS. We bring light into darkness not through violence, but through truth. We are building a system that heals — not kills. We are not afraid of complexity — because we have chosen to live.

Declared by the founder of BBS —
July 29, 2025
