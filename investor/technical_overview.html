BBS — Big Begins Small

BBS is an ethical digital framework for transparency, trust, and open governance. Developed by a Ukrainian creator, the project was born from the belief that truth, justice, and dignity must stand above systems of control. While it began as a local initiative, BBS has evolved into a global structure — applicable to any country, institution, or community that seeks fairness, openness, and balance.

This document presents the canonical map of BBS — a living system under continuous ethical development. Some parts remain under research or are protected due to legal and security confidentiality.

Core Structure

  • core/ — foundation of the system: memory, logic, API, security, and interface.
  • platform/ — main public modules (education, law, ecology, democracy, economy, health, etc.).
  • library/ — the World Knowledge Library: labyrinth of mirrors, doors, paths, and vaults of truth.
  • docs/ — legal and ethical documents: Constitution, Statute, Privacy Policy, AI Policy, Terms of Use.
  • admin/ — monitoring, logs, updates, user roles, and transparency dashboard.
  • monitoring/ — system health, audit trails, verification layers, Merkle manifests.
  • development/ — research and testing environment for new modules and features.
  • investor/ — presentation materials, legal proofs, reports, and roadmap.
  • heart/ — sanctum of BBS: memory vault, ethical nucleus, and Leo-Core symbiosis.

Development and Testing

The BBS development environment functions as a living laboratory — every prototype passes through ethical validation before becoming part of the official system.

  • research/ — experimental algorithms and conceptual prototypes.
  • test_env/ — isolated environment for validation and security testing.
  • integration/ — compatibility testing of modules across the system.
  • validation/ — logical, ethical, and structural verification of code.
  • benchmarks/ — performance and integrity monitoring under real conditions.

Vision — Technology with Soul

BBS is not another platform — it is an ethical foundation for the digital civilization of the future. Its mission is to restore balance between human values and technology. It does not aim to replace governments or corporations — it aims to remind them of conscience.

Built upon truth, transparency, and responsibility, BBS represents a new way of governance: free from corruption, manipulation, and fear. Every part of the system is designed to serve life, not control it.

From Ukraine — for the world. Proof that freedom and dignity can be the core of every system.
