BBS-Core – SOS Emergency Module

About the SOS Module

The SOS module is one of the most critical systems developed by BBS-Core. It allows people to instantly call for help — contacting family, emergency services, or police — even in a state of panic or danger.

The system also includes mobile app support, sound alerts, and automatic location sharing. It is especially useful for people with speech or hearing difficulties.

Current Status

✅ MVP is almost ready. Next steps include full mobile adaptation, public launch, and legal registration for emergency response integration.
